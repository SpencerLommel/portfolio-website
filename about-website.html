When I first made this portfolio website, I had a small amount of knowledge about html/js/css. Besides the websites I made back in middle & high school for fun and the hours spent looking around in the Chrome inspector panel I knew nothing. As I constructed this website I would visualize what I wanted it to look like and then when I didn't know how to accomplish that I would look at the MDN Docs or W3Schools and pick up on pieces that I could add to my website. In my opinion, this is the most amazing thing about programming. You don't need to know everything to create something you are happy with; you can research and build as you go. Over the next few months I will continue to improve this website and add new features/technologies.
